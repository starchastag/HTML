Definition and Uses

The <a> tag defines a hyperlinks , which is used to link from one page to another .

The most importatn attributes of the <a> element is the herf attributes , which indicates the link destination .

By default , link will appear as folllows in all browser:

  • An unvisited link is underline and blue.
  • A visited link is underline and purple .
  • An active link is underline and red.


Tips and Notes:

Tip: If the <a> tag has no herf attributes , it only a placeholder for a hyperlink.
